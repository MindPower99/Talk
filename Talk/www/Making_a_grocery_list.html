Making a Grocery List

Ruth is running out to the grocery store.
Before she goes, she checks if her roommate needs her to pick anything up.
Let’s review common products found at a grocery store.

Vocabulary

loaf: a bread that is shaped and baked in a single piece and can be sliced for eating
bread: a baked product used fro making sandwiches or toast
flour: a powder made from grains such as wheat, used for making bread
sugar: a sweet substance used to make food and drinks sweet
be out of: a condition in which you have no more of something
tube: a container that you squeeze something out of such as a toothpaste
jar: a glass or plastic container that is usually used for storing food
a dozen: twelve
dish detergent: soap that you use to wash plates, cups, and other dishes
cereal: a food that is made from grain and eaten with milk, especially in the morning
toothpast: a substance that you clean your teeth with
